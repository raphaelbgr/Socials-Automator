## Niche Selection Criteria

When choosing a niche, consider:

1. **Hashtag Volume**: Optimal niches show 500K-2M posts (not millions = oversaturated, not too few = no audience)
2. **Monetization Path**: Digital products, affiliates, sponsorships, coaching
3. **Content Sustainability**: Can you generate 3+ posts/day indefinitely?
4. **AI Generation Fit**: Does the content work well with AI text/image generation?
5. **Your Interest**: Long-term success requires genuine engagement with the topic
